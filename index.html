Confederation College proudly boasts state-of-the-art facilities and technology, offering a dynamic learning environment at our 8 campuses located throughout Northwestern Ontario (serving an area the size of France). Students can also enjoy our 140+ acres of park-like campus in the center of Thunder Bay, as well as the beautiful, natural surroundings across our regional campuses.
